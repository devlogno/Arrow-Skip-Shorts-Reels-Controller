# Privacy Policy for Arrow Skip

**Last Updated: April 30, 2026**

Arrow Skip ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

## 1. Data Collection
Arrow Skip **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**. 
- I do not track your browsing history.
- I do not collect information about the videos you watch.
- I do not have access to your account information on any website (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.).

## 2. Data Storage
All settings and configurations you create within the extension (such as custom keyboard shortcuts, skip durations, and site-specific speeds) are stored **locally** on your device using the `chrome.storage.sync` API.
- If you are logged into Chrome, this data may be synced across your devices by Google, but it is never accessible to the developer of this extension.

## 3. Use of Permissions
The extension requires certain permissions to function:
- **`storage`**: Used solely to save your personal settings.
- **`scripting`**: Used solely to interact with HTML5 `<video>` elements to enable speed and skip controls.
- **`host_permissions`**: Used solely to detect and control video elements on the websites you visit or specifically add to the extension.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
I do not share any information with third parties, as I do not collect any information in the first place.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues page.
