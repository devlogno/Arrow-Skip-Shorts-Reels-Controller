Take Control of Your Feed

Arrow Skip gives you professional-grade controls for short-form video platforms. Tired of being unable to skip forward or change playback speeds? Arrow Skip flips the script.

  • Precision Seeking: Use Arrow Keys (← →) to skip backward or forward with custom durations (1s to 60s).
  • Sticky Speed: Set a default playback speed per site that automatically applies as you scroll.
  • Hands-Free Auto-Scroll: Automatically jump to the next video the moment the current one finishes.
  • Smart Rate-Lock: Advanced logic prevents platforms from resetting your preferred playback speed.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

Arrow Skip is committed to your privacy. The extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

1. Data Collection

I do not track your browsing history, collect information about the videos you watch, or access your account information on any website (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.).

2. Data Storage

All settings and configurations—such as custom keyboard shortcuts, skip durations, and site-specific speeds—are stored locally on your device using the chrome.storage.sync API. If you are logged into Chrome, this may sync across your devices via Google, but it remains inaccessible to the developer.

3. Use of Permissions

  • Storage: Solely used to save your personal settings.
  • Scripting: Used to interact with HTML5 video elements to enable speed and skip controls.
  • Host Permissions: Used to detect and control video elements on the websites you visit or specifically add.

I do not share any information with third parties, as no information is collected in the first place.
